Split

Kevin

Kevin Wendell Crumb is the main antagonist of the 2017 live action film Split. He is a mysterious man who suffers from an extreme case of Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID), and has 23 separate personalities residing in his mind. Kevin and the majority of his personalities are by no means evil, however two of the most dominant—Dennis, an obsessive, cold and tough man, and Patricia, a manipulative female—are both aggressive and vicious in their own way.
